Timestamp Microservice

A Timestamp Microservice for FreeCodeCamp Api Projects

How to use it:

You can use a natural language date or a unix-timestamp to receive a timestamp and the natural date:

  • .../api/timestamp/2015-12-25
  • .../December 25, 2015
  • .../December 25 2015
  • .../Dec 25, 2015
  • .../Dec 25 2015
  • .../25 December 2015
  • .../25 December 15
  • .../25 Dec 2015
  • .../25 Dec 15

    • Or provide a unix-timestamp (milliseconds since January 1, 1970)

  • .../1451030400000

Output Example:


{
  unix: 1451030400000,
  utc: "Friday, December 25, 2015 00:00:00 GMT"
}
